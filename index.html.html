Profile

With over seven years of international experience, I lead BIM coordination and digital delivery on large-scale institutional, commercial, and infrastructure projects across the Middle East, Asia-Pacific, Europe, and North America.

My practice centers on ISO 19650-compliant workflows, managing Common Data Environments, and establishing quality frameworks that ensure model integrity from design development through construction and handover. I coordinate across architectural, structural, and MEP disciplines to resolve complex spatial conflicts, maintain constructability, and deliver accurate asset information.

This work requires precision in both technical execution and collaboration—authoring BIM Execution Plans, conducting rigorous QA/QC audits, and implementing automation that improves efficiency without compromising quality. The result is coordinated, data-rich models that support informed decision-making throughout the project lifecycle.
